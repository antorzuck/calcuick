OTE Compensation Guide

What is OTE (On-Target Earnings)?

On-Target Earnings represents the total compensation a sales professional can expect when achieving 100% of their quota, combining base salary with variable compensation.

OTE = Base\ Salary + Target\ Commission + Bonuses

Commission Structures

  • Flat Rate: Commission = Sales \times Rate
  • Tiered: Commission = \sum_{tier} Sales_{tier} \times Rate_{tier}
  • Accelerator: Commission = Base + (Excess \times Accelerator)
  • Draw Against: Net = max(0, Commission - Draw)

Key OTE Components

  • Base Salary: Fixed annual compensation
  • Variable Pay: Commission and performance bonuses
  • Quota: Sales target for full commission
  • Accelerators: Higher rates above quota

Optimization Strategies

  • Understand your commission structure and breakpoints
  • Focus on high-value opportunities near quota
  • Leverage accelerators for over-achievement
  • Plan territory coverage for maximum efficiency
  • Track performance metrics consistently
